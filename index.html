About Me

Teacher turned Developer

From Legos and painting to designing the coolest HTML for my Myspace page in highschool, I've always been drawn to building and creating. I attended CSU Chico and became a teacher so that I could share my love of learning with the world.

During the pandemic when teaching went remote, I began learning new software so quickly and efficiently that I was soon leading professional development on it for the other teachers on my campus. This got me thinking, what if I pursued my love of technology and transitioned to tech?

After 6 months of exploring coding for fun in my free time, I am now enrolled in a full stack coding bootcamp learning HTML, CSS, Javascript, Python, SQL, Mongo, JSON, PostgreSQL, MongoDB, Express.js, Bootstrap, Django, React and more. I am on track to graduate on August 29th, 2023. Check out some of my recent projects below.
